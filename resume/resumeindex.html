About Me

Hello! I am Kaia, a Seminole State College student based in Oviedo, FL. Majoring in Digital Media, I aspire to be a storyboard artist for animated projects and cartoons. My passion and hobby of creating art was built upon and improved through storyboarding classes hosted by Thomas Thorspecken, a Disney artist and animator for films like Brother Bear and Tarzan.

I have done primarily art for my own enjoyment, but am branching out to find opportunities and connect with those that could use my assistance in making a project that fits their artistic visions.
